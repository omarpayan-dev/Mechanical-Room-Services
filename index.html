Reliable Solutions for Businesses, Schools, and Facilities

How Customers Are Supported

With over 12 years in HVAC and plumbing service sales across Central Texas—covering schools, military sites, multifamily, and healthcare—customers rely on Omar Payan to simplify the process and get results. Support focuses on arranging the right services at the right time: preventative maintenance to avoid downtime, responsive repairs when problems arise, and managed replacements when equipment reaches end‑of‑life.

Services at a Glance

  • Rooftop Units (RTUs)
    • Preventative maintenance coordinated for peak performance
    • Repairs scheduled quickly to minimize disruption
    • Replacements managed end‑to‑end, including crane lifts and controls integration
  • Commercial Water Heaters
    • Routine inspections and maintenance for safe, reliable hot water
    • Repairs arranged with proper parts and fittings
    • Replacements guided for efficiency and code compliance (pumps, neutralizers, venting)
  • Plumbing & MedGas
    • Preventative checks including drain/sewer inspections
    • Emergency repairs coordinated for fast response
    • MedGas work delivered with certified installers/inspectors to NFPA standards
  • Boilers
    • Scheduled maintenance for safety and efficiency
    • Repairs coordinated to restore service quickly
    • Replacements planned for redundancy and modern efficiency
  • Chillers
    • PM programs including coil cleaning, refrigerant checks, and pump inspections
    • Diagnostics and repairs arranged promptly
    • Replacements managed from selection through installation
