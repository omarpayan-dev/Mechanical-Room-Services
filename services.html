Reliable Solutions for Businesses, Schools, and Facilities

How Customers Are Supported

With over 12 years of HVAC and plumbing service sales experience in Central Texas—covering schools, military facilities, multifamily, and healthcare—customers rely on Omar Payan to coordinate the right services at the right time. The focus is simple: preventative maintenance to avoid downtime, responsive repairs when problems arise, and guided replacements when equipment reaches end‑of‑life.

Rooftop Units (RTUs)

Preventative maintenance programs are coordinated to keep RTUs at peak performance. If issues surface, repairs are scheduled quickly to minimize disruption. When replacement is needed, projects are managed end‑to‑end—crane lifts, staging, and controls integration—so the building stays comfortable and operational.

Commercial Water Heaters

Routine inspections and maintenance extend system life and keep hot water reliable. When repairs are required, technicians arrive with the right parts and fittings. For replacements, options are presented that balance efficiency and cost, with coordination for pumps, neutralizers, and venting to ensure code compliance.

Plumbing & MedGas

Preventative plumbing checks—drain and sewer inspections—reduce unexpected failures. Emergency repairs are coordinated quickly when needed. For MedGas scopes, certified installers and third‑party inspectors are engaged to meet NFPA standards, delivering clean tie‑ins and compliant upgrades for clinical spaces.

Boilers

Scheduled maintenance keeps boilers safe and efficient. When problems occur, repair crews are coordinated to restore service promptly. Replacement planning addresses efficiency, redundancy, and safe, code‑compliant installations tailored to each facility.

Chillers

Preventative maintenance includes coil care, refrigerant checks, and pump inspections to stay ready for Texas heat. Diagnostics and repairs are arranged quickly when performance drops. Replacement projects are managed from equipment selection through installation to ensure long‑term reliability.
