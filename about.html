About Omar Payan

Consulting for Commercial HVAC & Plumbing — Central Texas

Who I Am

Facilities-Minded, Consultant Approach

Based in Central Texas, I work as a facilities-minded consultant for HVAC and plumbing decisions. Over 12+ years across San Antonio, New Braunfels, San Marcos, and Austin, I’ve supported schools, military sites, healthcare, worship, multifamily, and commercial offices. My time as a U.S. Air Force Staff Sergeant shaped how I operate: disciplined planning, clear communication, and steady follow-through.

Each engagement starts with a concise discovery and site walk to understand goals, risks, budget, schedules, and constraints—comfort complaints, hot-water reliability, controls limits, access and safety, and the rhythms of the space. Major assets are baselined (model/serial, capacity, age, condition, criticality) with photos and notes. From there, I help you navigate side-by-side options—what you want and what the building needs—with brand-agnostic recommendations and plain tradeoffs on cost, downtime, lead times, energy, and compliance.

Priorities are practical: fix the pain points first to restore comfort, hot water, or critical service. Preventative maintenance is prioritized where it prevents repeat failures and fits the budget; repairs are scoped and scheduled with clear pricing and windows; replacements are proposed only when safety, reliability, or total cost of ownership make the case. When a change-out is justified, I manage the steps—selection, crane day, controls integration, commissioning—and reuse viable components where it makes sense. Work is planned around your operations, safety protocols are non-negotiable (permits, LOTO, hot work, MedGas), and close-out includes readings/photos and next steps so decisions stay simple and you’re never guessing about what comes next.

How Customers Are Supported
